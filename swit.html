Our Server Story

Welcome to FiveM Community, a server born from a passion for immersive roleplay and a vision to create something truly special in the FiveM universe. Our journey began in 2020 when a small group of dedicated players noticed a gap in the market for a server that balanced serious roleplay with accessibility for newcomers.

Over the years, we've grown from a modest 10-player server to a thriving community with hundreds of active members. Our unique economic system, custom jobs, and deep character development mechanics set us apart from other roleplay servers.

What makes our community special is our commitment to quality over quantity. We carefully vet our members to ensure everyone is dedicated to creating compelling stories and respecting the roleplay experience. Our dedicated staff team works tirelessly to maintain a balanced and fair environment where every player has the opportunity to thrive.

As we look to the future, we're excited to announce our upcoming expansion featuring new districts, professions, and gameplay mechanics that will take the FiveM experience to unprecedented heights. Join us on this incredible journey and become part of our story.

Server Rules

General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind
  • English only in global chat
  • No advertising other servers or communities
  • Follow all instructions from staff members

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in character at all times while roleplaying
  • No metagaming (using OOC information in-character)
  • No powergaming (forcing actions on other players)
  • FearRP - value your character's life appropriately
  • New Life Rule - if your character dies, you lose memory of events leading to death

Gameplay Rules

  • No cheating, exploiting, or hacking
  • No random deathmatching (RDM)
  • No vehicle deathmatching (VDM)
  • No combat logging during active roleplay situations
  • Follow all server-specific mechanics and systems

Voice Chat Rules

  • Use push-to-talk in crowded areas
  • No background noise, music, or soundboards
  • Keep voice chat clear and audible
  • Respect others in voice channels
  • No screaming or excessively loud volumes
