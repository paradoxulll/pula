Our Story

Welcome to Swit Community, a server born from a passion for immersive roleplay and a vision to create something truly special in the FiveM universe. Our journey began in 2020 when a small group of dedicated players noticed a gap in the market for a server that balanced serious roleplay with accessibility for newcomers.

Over the years, we've grown from a modest 10-player server to a thriving community with hundreds of active members. Our unique economic system, custom jobs, and deep character development mechanics set us apart from other roleplay servers.

What makes our community special is our commitment to quality over quantity. We carefully vet our members to ensure everyone is dedicated to creating compelling stories and respecting the roleplay experience.

Staff Rules

FiveM Server Rules

Respect all players and staff members at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech will not be tolerated.

No cheating, exploiting, or using any unauthorized mods or scripts that provide an unfair advantage.

Roleplay must be realistic and immersive at all times. Stay in character and follow server lore.

No random deathmatch (RDM) or vehicle deathmatch (VDM). All hostile actions must have proper roleplay justification.

Follow all server-specific roleplay guidelines and scenarios. Listen to staff instructions during events.

No advertising other servers or communities in any form, including in-game, Discord, or through direct messages.

Keep voice chat clear of background noise and use it appropriately for roleplay situations.

Do not abuse game mechanics or exploit bugs. Report any issues to staff immediately.

Respect the roleplay of others and do not interrupt or disrupt ongoing scenarios without proper context.

Discord Community Rules

Be respectful to all community members and staff. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

No spamming, flooding, or excessive use of @mentions. Keep conversations constructive and on-topic.

Keep discussions in their appropriate channels. Read channel descriptions before posting.

No NSFW, offensive, or inappropriate content including images, links, or discussions.

Do not share personal information of yourself or others. Protect your privacy and the privacy of fellow members.

Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

No advertising or self-promotion without staff approval.

Use English as the primary language in public channels to ensure everyone can participate.

Respect staff decisions and follow their instructions. If you have concerns, address them through proper channels.

Staff Team Guidelines

Always lead by example and maintain professionalism in all interactions with community members.

Remain impartial and fair in all decisions and interactions. Personal relationships should not influence staff actions.

Do not abuse administrative powers or privileges. Use tools and permissions only for their intended purposes.

Maintain confidentiality regarding staff discussions, decisions, and sensitive community information.

Be active and responsive to community needs, reports, and questions in a timely manner.

Follow the established chain of command for issues, appeals, and important decisions.

Document actions and decisions appropriately for transparency and accountability.

Continuously work to improve your knowledge of server rules, procedures, and roleplay standards.

Support fellow staff members and maintain a positive, collaborative team environment.
